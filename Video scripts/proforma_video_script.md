# NobleInvoice: 60-Second Proforma Video Script

**Target AI Generator:** Google VEO 3 / Notebook LLM / Synthesia  
**Tone:** Professional, authoritative, sleek, fast-paced  
**Target Audience:** Freelancers, agency owners, B2B consultants, international sellers  
**Visual Style:** Modern glassmorphism UI, high-contrast dark mode elements, dynamic screen transitions.

---

## 🎬 Script & Prompt Guide

### [0:00 - 0:10] The Hook (The Pain)
**Visual Prompt for VEO 3:** 
> *A split screen. On the left, a frustrated business owner staring at a messy, broken Microsoft Word table filled with miscalculated numbers. On the right, a sleek, modern smartphone screen showing a pristine NobleInvoice dashboard. Fast motion, cinematic lighting.*

**Voiceover (Confident & Relatable):**
"Still using Word or Excel to send proforma invoices? Stop. You're bleeding time and credibility."

---

### [0:10 - 0:25] The Problem with Legacy Methods
**Visual Prompt for VEO 3:** 
> *A montage of confusing email threads flying across the screen. Words like 'Version_3_Final.docx' and 'Customs Rejected' flash in red typography. Smooth zoom-in on an international shipping box.*

**Voiceover:**
"When you rely on static templates, a single revision turns into a version control nightmare. And if you're shipping internationally, one missing HS code or currency error on your proforma means customs delays and lost money."

---

### [0:25 - 0:45] The NobleInvoice Solution
**Visual Prompt for VEO 3:** 
> *Screen recording simulation: A smooth, animated cursor clicks 'Create Proforma' on the NobleInvoice web app. The interface is deep blue and bright white. The cursor adds a client, selects 'USD', and a beautiful PDF generates instantly. 3D floating checkmarks appear.*

**Voiceover:**
"Enter NobleInvoice. Create a world-class, legally compliant proforma invoice in under 60 seconds. Our platform automatically handles multi-currency formatting, customs-ready declarations, and exact terms. It looks beautiful, and it's practically error-proof."

---

### [0:45 - 0:55] The "Aha!" Moment
**Visual Prompt for VEO 3:** 
> *The screen shows the generated Proforma Invoice. A glowing blue button appears at the top that says "Convert to Final Invoice". The cursor clicks it, and the document magically morphs into a formal Commercial Invoice with a "PAID" stamp hitting the screen.*

**Voiceover:**
"The best part? When the client approves the estimate, convert your proforma into a legally binding tax invoice with a single click. Zero double data entry."

---

### [0:55 - 1:00] The CTA
**Visual Prompt for VEO 3:** 
> *The NobleInvoice logo appears on a sleek, dark background. A search bar appears typing "NobleInvoice Proforma", then a button pulses saying "Start Invoicing Free".*

**Voiceover:**
"Stop estimating. Start operating like a global enterprise. Create your free proforma invoice today at NobleInvoice.com."

---

## 💡 Tips for Notebook LLM / Voice Generation
*   **Voice Type:** Select a voice that sounds like a seasoned B2B tech founder—authoritative, clear, and slightly upbeat. 
*   **Pacing:** Ensure there is a slight pause (0.5 seconds) right before "Enter NobleInvoice" for dramatic effect.
*   **Music Bed:** Use a driving, modern electronic or lo-fi corporate track underneath. It shouldn't distract, but it should keep the energy high.
