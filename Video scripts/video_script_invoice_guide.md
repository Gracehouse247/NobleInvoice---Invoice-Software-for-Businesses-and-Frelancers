# Video Script: How to Make an Invoice That Actually Gets Paid

**Target Platform:** YouTube / Page Embed  
**Target Duration:** 60-75 seconds  
**Tone:** Professional, direct, authoritative, high-energy (like a premium SaaS commercial)  
**Generation Tool:** Google VEO 3 / Notebook LLM  
**Visual Style:** Fast-paced cuts, sleek 3D UI mockups, dark-mode aesthetics with electric blue highlights.

---

### [0:00 - 0:08] The Hook (Pattern Interrupt)
**Visual:** 
A split screen. On the left, someone looking stressed staring at a clunky Excel spreadsheet. On the right, a sleek, modern smartphone interface showing the NobleInvoice dashboard with a green "$5,500 Paid" notification popping up.
**Voiceover (Confident, crisp):** 
"You just finished the work. Now you have to ask for the money. If you're still using Word or Excel to do that, you're secretly telling your clients that you're an amateur."

### [0:08 - 0:18] The Agitation
**Visual:** 
Close-up of a generic, ugly PDF invoice. Big red stamps slam down on it: "NO PO NUMBER", "MISSING TAX ID", "MANUAL BANK TRANSFER". The paper crumples and gets tossed away.
**Voiceover:** 
"Corporate finance teams hate messy invoices. If you miss a PO number or forget your tax ID, your invoice goes straight to the bottom of the pile. You wait 30 days. Then 60 days. Then you're sending those awkward follow-up emails."

### [0:18 - 0:35] The Solution (NobleInvoice)
**Visual:** 
Fast, dynamic screen recording of the NobleInvoice app. 
1. User taps "New Invoice".
2. Client info auto-fills instantly. 
3. User adds a line item for "Brand Strategy".
4. Tax calculates automatically.
**Voiceover:** 
"Here's how professionals do it. With NobleInvoice, you don't build an invoice. You generate a payment engine. In under 60 seconds, you select your client, add your items, and the app handles the tax math automatically."

### [0:35 - 0:48] The "Aha!" Moment (Information Gain)
**Visual:** 
The screen focuses heavily on the "Pay Now" button embedded right inside the digital invoice. A cursor clicks it, and an Apple Pay / Credit Card sheet slides up smoothly. 
**Voiceover:** 
"But here is the secret weapon: Don't ask them to wire you money. NobleInvoice embeds a secure, one-click payment button directly inside the invoice. Apple Pay, credit cards, global transfers. They click, you get paid."

### [0:48 - 0:58] The Proof
**Visual:** 
Three fast-paced text overlays with cinematic sound effects (whoosh/boom):
- "61% Higher Corporate Approval"
- "Paid 14 Days Faster"
- "Zero Manual Errors"
**Voiceover:** 
"The result? Invoices sent with NobleInvoice get approved 61% faster, and are settled two weeks sooner than traditional PDFs."

### [0:58 - 1:05] The CTA
**Visual:** 
The NobleInvoice logo in metallic silver/blue on a dark background. A glowing button appears: "Create Free Invoice Online".
**Voiceover:** 
"Your next invoice should take 60 seconds, not 60 minutes. Click the link below to create your first free invoice right now."

If you are passing this into VEO 3 for generation, use this exact prompt structure:
> *"Generate a cinematic, high-end SaaS commercial. The visual style should be 'dark mode glassmorphism' with vibrant blue and emerald green accents. Fast, snappy cuts. The UI mockups should look like a highly polished iOS/Web app. Keep the voiceover pacing energetic but authoritative, similar to an Apple product reveal."*
