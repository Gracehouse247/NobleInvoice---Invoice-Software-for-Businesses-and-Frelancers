**Tone:** Confident, premium, sophisticated, authoritative.
**Theme:** Elevating your checkout experience, building unquestionable trust, and getting paid 2x faster.

### Production Notes for Voiceover & Pacing
1. **Music Track:** Start with a low, slightly tense electronic hum during the first 15 seconds. Transition to a confident, premium, warm ambient electronic beat at 0:15 that keeps a steady, prestigious tempo.
2. **VO Delivery:** The speaker should sound calm, professional, and slightly conversational—not like a loud salesman, but like a trusted partner who understands the business owner's daily headaches.
3. **Typography on Screen:** Use clean, sans-serif fonts matching the website's brand identity. Focus numbers (e.g., **2x faster**, **₦0 Outstanding**) should be bold, massive, and clean.
