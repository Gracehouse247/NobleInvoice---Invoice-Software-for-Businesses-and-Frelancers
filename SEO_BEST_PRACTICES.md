# NobleInvoice SEO Best Practices

These rules must be strictly adhered to whenever the autonomous SEO engine (or AI agent) is generating new blog posts or landing/service pages.

## Core Rules

1. **SEO Title**: 
   - Must be less than 60 characters.
   - Must contain the focus keyword.
   - Please use the keyword evenly.

2. **Meta Description**: 
   - Must be less than 160 characters.
   - Must contain the focus keyword.
   - Please use the keyword evenly.

3. **URL Slug**: 
   - Must be less than 75 characters.
   - Must contain the focus keyword.

4. **Image Alt Tag**: 
   - Use the exact focus keyword as the image alt tag.

5. **Headings (H2/H3)**: 
   - The focus keyword must appear in at least one of the H2 or H3 headings.
   - Please use it evenly throughout the content.

*Note: The term "focus keyword" is synonymous with "target keyword".*
